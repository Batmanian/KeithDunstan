APITS

Published 11 Nov 1990

In this tenth chapter of the first book of memoirs, 'No Brains at All', Keith recalls the three decades he wrote a daily column for Melbourne's Sun News Pictorial newspaper. 'A place in the Sun' was the name of that column, or APITS.
